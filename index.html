Practical rules

01Protein is non-negotiable. 215g every single day — the #1 lever for muscle preservation.
02Creatine: 5g daily, keep taking it. Intracellular water, not fat. Stopping costs performance for no benefit.
03Rest days: cut 100–150g carbs. Keep protein and fat identical. Floor is 55g fat/day.
042-week stall: drop 100 kcal from carbs only. Never below 1,950 kcal total.
05Refeed every 2 weeks: ~2,850 kcal, all extra from carbs. Same protein, same fat. Not a cheat day.
0610,000 steps/day — ~270 kcal net above your 4k baseline. Zero recovery cost. Best tool you have.
