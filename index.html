Practical rules

01Protein is non-negotiable. Hit 215g every single day. This is the single biggest lever for muscle preservation during a cut.
02Creatine: 5g daily, keep taking it. The extra water it holds is intracellular — not fat. Stopping it costs strength and muscle performance for zero benefit.
03Rest days drop to recommended rest kcal. Cut carbs, keep protein and fat identical. Never go below 55g fat per day.
04If weight stalls for 2 weeks: drop calories by 100 kcal from carbs only. Never drop below 1,950 kcal total. Never cut protein.
05Refeed every 2 weeks: one day at ~2,850 kcal, all extra from carbs. Same protein, same fat. Not a cheat day.
0610,000 steps/day is your best fat-loss tool — zero recovery cost, ~270 kcal net extra burn vs your previous 4k average.
